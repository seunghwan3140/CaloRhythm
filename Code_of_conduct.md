# Code of Conduct

To maintain a healthy and welcoming environment for all contributors,  
the following rules apply across the entire CaloRhythm community,  
including Issues, Discussions, and Pull Requests.

---

## ✔ Respectful Communication
- Treat all contributors with **respect, courtesy, and professionalism**.
- Disrespectful behavior, personal attacks, or insulting remarks are not allowed.

---

## ✔ No Discrimination or Harassment
We do not tolerate:
- Harassment or hate speech  
- Discrimination based on gender, race, nationality, disability, or any personal attribute  
- Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome  

---

## ✔ Constructive Collaboration
- Provide helpful, actionable feedback.
- Critique code or ideas — *not* people.
- Assume good intentions from others and work toward shared improvement.

---

## ✔ Conflict Resolution
If disagreements arise:
- Remain calm and respectful.  
- Discuss concerns openly and constructively.  
- Maintainers may step in to moderate or make final decisions if needed.

---

## ✔ Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Warnings  
- Temporary or permanent restrictions from participation  
- Removal of inappropriate content  

These actions are taken at the discretion of the maintainers.

---

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct  
and help foster a supportive, productive community for everyone.
